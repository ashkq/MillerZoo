<h3>Interesting Facts</h3>
<ul>
  <li>We partner with Seafood Watch to help people make better seafood choices for healthy ocean life.</li>
  <li>Miller Park Zoo is handicap accessible</li>
  <li>Miller Park Zoo is an accredited member of the Association of Zoos and Aquariums, providing excellent care for animals, a great experience for you, and a better future for all living things.</li>
  <li>Zoo is open all year round</li>
  <li>It is also the only Illinois zoo to feature the endangered Sumatran tiger.</li>
</ul>
<h3>History</h3>
<p>Opened in the late 1800's, this city zoo began to house exotic animals after a traveling circus left behind a lion cub. Located on Historic Route 66, the zoo became a popular attraction among travelers.</p>
<h4>Size and visitors</h4>
<p>Miller Zoo spans a total of 4 acres with an annual 123,129 visitors a year!</p>
